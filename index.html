About Us

Healing Harbor Health is a Minnesota Basic Home Care agency dedicated to supporting seniors and individuals with disabilities in the comfort of their own homes. We believe every person deserves safety, dignity, and compassionate care.

Our founder is a disabled individual himself, which gives our agency a unique and personal understanding of the challenges, strengths, and resilience of the disability community. This lived experience shapes our mission, values, and commitment to exceptional, person-centered care.

Our Services

  • ✔ Assistance with Activities of Daily Living (ADLs)
  • ✔ Bathing, grooming, and personal hygiene
  • ✔ Help with mobility and transfers
  • ✔ Meal planning and preparation
  • ✔ Medication reminders
  • ✔ Light housekeeping
  • ✔ Companionship and supervision
  • ✔ Safety monitoring and fall prevention

Why Choose Us?

  • ✔ Minnesota Department of Health (MDH) Basic Home Care Licensed
  • ✔ Care delivered with dignity, respect, and cultural sensitivity
  • ✔ Experience supporting seniors and individuals with disabilities
  • ✔ Tailored care plans built around your needs and preferences
  • ✔ Reliable, trained, and compassionate caregivers

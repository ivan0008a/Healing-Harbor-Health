About Us

Who We Are

Healing Harbor Health is a Minnesota Basic Home Care agency dedicated to walking alongside seniors and people living with disabilities. Our goal is simple: to make home a safe, calm, and supportive harbor.

Our founder is a disabled individual himself. That lived experience means we understand not only the practical needs of daily care, but also the emotional realities—loss of independence, frustration, and the courage it takes to ask for help. This perspective shapes everything we do.

We partner with families, case managers, and community organizations to build person-centered care plans that respect each client’s preferences, routines, and cultural background.

Home Care Services We Provide

Healing Harbor Health focuses on non-medical Basic Home Care services allowed under Minnesota Department of Health Basic Home Care licensing.

Personal Care & ADLs

  • Bathing, grooming, hygiene
  • Dressing and undressing
  • Toileting assistance
  • Mobility & safe transfers

Daily Living Support

  • Meal planning & preparation
  • Light housekeeping & laundry
  • Medication reminders
  • Errands and accompaniment

Companionship & Safety

  • Friendly visits & conversation
  • Safety checks & fall prevention
  • Routine & structure support
  • Caregiver respite for families

Person-Centered Planning

  • Individualized care plans
  • Coordination with case managers
  • Support for disability-related needs
  • Culturally respectful care

Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve communities in Minnesota. Please contact us with your city and ZIP code so we can confirm whether we currently serve your area.

Do you accept waiver programs or state-funded services?

We are waiver-friendly and happy to work with case managers and county agencies. We will walk you through options and documentation needed.

Is Healing Harbor Health medically licensed?

We operate as a Minnesota Basic Home Care agency and focus on non-medical services such as ADL support, supervision, safety, and companionship. We do not provide skilled nursing or medical procedures.

Can you help my family create a care plan?

Yes. We collaborate with you, the client, and any care team members to design a person-centered plan that reflects your goals, routines, and preferences.
